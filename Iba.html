Institute of Business Administration.


  • The Institute of Business Administration (IBA), Karachi; (انسٹيٹيوٹ آف بزنس ايڈمنسٹريشن) is a public university in Karachi,
    Sindh, Pakistan. Admission to IBA KARACHI is based on Entry Test, SAT/ACT and Interview.At undergraduate
    level, IBA offers degrees in Business Administration, Accounting and Finance, Computer Science, Economics,
    Economics and Mathematics, Social Sciences and Liberal Arts. The graduate programs offered by the IBA include
    degrees in Business Administration, MBA Executive, Computer Science, Economics, Islamic Banking and Finance,
    Journalism, Management, Data Sciences, Finance and Mathematics.
  • The doctoral programs offered at the Institute include degrees in Computer Science, Economics and Mathematics.
    IBA was accredited by the South Asian Quality Assurance System (SAQS) in 2011. CFA University Partner and the
    first university in Pakistan to be granted that status by the CFA Institute.The Institute of Business Administration,
    IBA Karachi is an unconstrained university in Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan.

History.

It was established in the year 1955 and is one of the oldest business schools outside North America.
At the present time, IBA is one of Pakistan’s renowned and top-ranked institutes ideally known for its
business program. It is one of the two Pakistani business schools being awarded the certificate
by the South Asian Quality System. IBA’s campus covers the zone of 750,000 sq. ft and has around 6000
total enrollments. IBA collaborates with 15 institutions throughout the world.
There has been a continuous increase from 30% Ph.D.’s in 2008, to over 50% till now. More than 20 faculty
members are presently pursuing their Ph.D.’s at top leading foreign universities, controlling IBA’s
principal position with a faculty of over 70% PhDs in the coming time. The motto of IBA Karachi is “Leadership and Ideas for Tomorrow”.
