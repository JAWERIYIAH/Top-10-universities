Top 10 universities of pakistan.

1. NUST

National University of Computer Science and technology
(NUST) is located in Islamabad. Founded in 1991. It was
Founded to provide quality technical education. Nust offers
undergraduate and graduate degrees, including doctoral and
professional degrees.

2. LUMS

Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS) is a
private research university, located in Lahore, Punjab,
Pakistan. LUMS provides 18 undergraduate majors and
24 graduate degrees. This university is best for bussiness
studies and marketing purpose.

3. FAST

The National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences
(Initials: NUCES) also commonly known as "Foundation for
Advancement of Science and Technology" (FAST), is private
research university with multiple campuses in different
cities of Pakistan.

4. IBA

Institute of Business Administration (IBA) is a public university
in Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan. Admission to IBA in karachi is
based on Entry Test, SAT/ACT and Interview. IBA is an
unconstrained university.

5. COMSATS

The COMSATS University Islamabad (CUI), formerly known
as COMSATS Institute of Information Technology (CIIT), is a
public university in Pakistan. It is a multi-campus university with
its principal seat located in Islamabad.

6. AGA Khan

Aga Khan University is a non-profit institution and an agency
of the Aga Khan Development Network. It was Founded in 1983
as Pakistan's first private university. Starting in 2000. Founded
in 1983. The Aga Khan University is an international university.

7. Quaid-e-Azam

Quaid-i-Azam University Islamabad commonly referred to as
(QAU), founded as University of Islamabad, is a ranked 1 public
research university in Islamabad, Pakistan. The university has, as of
2015, grown into the largest varsity in Islamabad with a total
enrollment exceeding 13,000 students.

8. NED

The NED University of Engineering & Technology is a public
university located in the urban area of Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan.
It is one of the oldest and best engineering universities in Pakistan,
acknowledged for its best teaching practices and graduates.

9. Taxila

The University of Engineering and Technology, Taxila is a public
university located in Taxila, Punjab, Pakistan. It was established
in 1975 as a campus of the University of Engineering and Technology,
Lahore and chartered as an independent university in 1993.
It offers bachelor's, master's and doctoral degrees in engineering
and applied sciences.

10. IST

The Institute of Space Technology (also known as IST), is a public
university located in Islamabad, Pakistan. It is focused on the study
of astronomy, aerospace engineering and astronautics.nstitute of
Space Technology on 21 November 2013, launched Pakistan's first
Cubesat satellite, iCUBE-1.
